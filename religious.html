Tal Barahi Temple, located on an island, Accessible by boat, the temple provides stunning views of the Annapurna Range and the surrounding mountains. The goddess Barahi is worshipped for her protective and nurturing qualities, making Tal Barahi Temple a cultural and spiritual landmark in the enchanting city of Pokhara.
